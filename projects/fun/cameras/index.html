Security Camera Photography

Watching the watchers.

<-- Back to Fun

Security Camera Photography has always been a big interest due to the Cyberpunk nature of the photos. Urban, desolate areas littered with cameras are the perfect setting for a cool Cyberpunk movie, and I've alweays had an attraction to those kinds of areas. I've always had an interest in observing the observers.

Noticing things like how many cameras cover an area, whether they're fake or real, and other physical defenses can tell you a lot about an area. Why does a random alleyway have dozens of cameras and motion-activated flood lights? Not sure, maybe there's something fun there...

While I don't have many photos at the moment, I'll be adding more as I find them on my various phones and hard drives :)
