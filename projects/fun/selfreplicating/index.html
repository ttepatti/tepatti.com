Self Replicating Hardware

"It goes it goes it goes it goes it goes" - Stefan Corbin Burnett

<-- Back to Fun

Self-replicating hardware has always been an interesting topic to me. You take all of the properties of your conventional computer virus and try to implement them in real life! Obviously this has been possible with actual diseases and biological viruses for billions of years, but for a physical and non-living object to re-create itself would be quite interesting.

There has been much study on the topic, and to be quite honest most of it goes above my head. I would love to understand more about it, and I'll update here as I research it more. For now, you'll have to excuse my sparse content and heavy use of links without comment.
