Custom Car Lights

100% Japanese-certified seizures on the roadway

<-- Back to Hardware

I've been interested in making custom head lights ever since I saw videos of Japanese drift cars with head light flashers installed. Japanese drifters commonly switch out foglights and headlights with colored LED lights and add flashing circuits to them, making quite the lightshow as they drift.

Another awesome innovation is putting LED screens in the tail lights, enabling scrolling text, pictures, and other shenanigans. I think my EF Civic's long tail light bar would be perfect for a project like this.

I'm still planning out the hardware I would need and the type of lights I could use, so this isn't fully updated yet. Expect more updates in the near future! :)
